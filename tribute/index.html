Here's a time line of Subhas Chandra Bose's Life :

  • 23 Jan 1897 - Subhas Chandra Bose was born.
  • 01 Feb 1941 - Subhas Chandra Bose arrived in Kabul, Afghanistan.
  • 02 Apr 1941 - Subhash Chandra Bose arrived in Berlin, Germany.
  • 08 Feb 1943 - Subhash Chandra Bose departed Germany for Japan via submarine.
  • 11 May 1943 - Subhash Chandra Bose arrived in Tokyo, Japan.
  • 02 Jul 1943 - Subhash Chandra Bose arrived in Singapore.
  • 04 Jul 1943 - Subhash Chandra Bose took command of the Indian National Army and assumed the leadership position of the Indian Independence League.
  • 20 Aug 1945 - Subhash Chandra Bose was cremated in the main crematorium of Taihoku (now Taipei), Taiwan.
  • 23 Aug 1945 - Japanese news agency Do Trzei announced the death of Subhash Chandra Bose.
  • 07 Sep 1945 - Subhash Chandra Bose's ashes were brought to Tokyo, Japan by Lieutenant Tatsuo Hayashida.
  • 08 Sep 1945 - Rama Murti, president of the Tokyo Indian Independence League, received Subhash Chandra Bose's ashes in Tokyo, Japan.
  • 14 Sep 1945 - A memorial for Subhash Chandra Bose was held in Tokyo, Japan.
